Past Projects

Trust for the Future served as a non-profit incubator for many great projects that went on to become their own successful organizations, including:


• Initiative for Environmental Sensitivity in Construction (founded
1990 by Charlie Howell)

• Descendents Day (founded 1996 by Charlie Howell)

• Clean Cities of Middle Tennessee (founded 2002 by Dave Pelton) (now
"Middle Tennessee Clean Fuels Coalition")
http://middletncleanfuels.org/

• Blessed Earth (founded 2005 by Matthew and Nancy Sleeth)
http://www.blessedearth.org/

• Urban Green Lab (founded 2010 by Dan Heller)
http://urbangreenlab.org/
