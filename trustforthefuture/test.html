Do you have a project you want to get started? Are you ready to fill out a proposal for non-profit sponsorship from Trust for the Future? Fill out this online form, click submit, then we will review and get in touch with you. We recommend you do your writing in a saved word document, then cut and paste your responses in the respective spaces below.

Proposal for Project Sponsorship

Please use the following format to propose a project for sponsorship by Trust for the Future.  We encourage a concise narrative.

  1. Describe your project.  Please include the following:
  • the mission, vision and purpose for the proposed project (50 word max)
  • specific measurable objectives for your project that you hope to achieve for your community at large (100 word max)
  • the strategies or methods to reach your objectives (100 word max)
  • projected budget (annual expenses and revenues)
  • a proposed timeline outlining how you will begin your project in the first one to three years (50 word max)

  1. Describe the community that your project will serve.  Please include the following:
  • geographic and demographic community (50 word max)
  • describe the needs of the target population that you serve (50 word max) 
  • list other organizations that address similar needs (50 word max)

  1. Describe the benefits your project will provide the community within the first three years (100 word max).

  1. Describe achievements to-date: work and successes in the research, design, piloting or demonstration of the project
    (100 word max).

  1. List key relationships you currently have with community allies, project partners, funders and advisors.  Please
    include a brief description of the role of each (a few words for each):

  1. What, if any, additional organizations or groups do you need to know/work with in order to make your project
    a success (50 word max)?

  1. How does your work-related, volunteer or other experience prepare you to succeed in the proposed project
    (100 word max)?

  1. Outline your specific expectations regarding the benefit(s) a relationship with Trust for the Future will provide
    your project (50 word max).
