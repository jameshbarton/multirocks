Parents:

Thank you for visiting this website. Parents are a very important part of the learning process and we greatly appreciate you taking the time to check out what your child will be learning.

What the site can do for you:

If you are one of those pesky parents who hate going over the same multiplication tables with your child every week, this site may just be your new best friend. By logging in and practicing their multiplication tables, your child will be able to learn the same mathematic functions without having to painfully write out every single one. Also, they will not have to go to you for help with practicing them. If you have any ideas on what other useful information should be included on this website, please do not hesitate to let me know.
